Creative
Experience Design Lead
Singapore
south

This role is 50% hands-on design, 50% direction/strategy and forms part of our studio design team based in Singapore, answering directly to the Head of Design.

Experience Designers play a crucial role in all aspects of the digital solutions we build – working directly with our client’s senior management teams to define the digital experience, researching user behaviour, presenting and executing design concepts.

In addition, you will provide strong direction and leadership to our studio designers, educating and guiding them through the design process.

Responsibilities

  • Provide estimation, design planning and design strategy for a range of digital projects
  • Lead client discussions, propose a broad range of design solutions across various digital platforms e.g. Web, App, WeChat, large screen, etc.
  • Present UX to our client’s mid/top-level management teams, justify and support your ideas with research where appropriate
  • Write clear design briefs and provide detailed design direction to team members
  • Create interface wireframes/concepts/prototypes, that incorporate the latest trends, adhere to UX best practices, and meet user/client needs
  • Define, develop, and maintain design systems across products
  • Direct and critique other design team members, providing feedback and leading by example
  • Evangelize user-centered design, design standards, and best practices
  • Help grow design capabilities and resources within our organization

Requirements

  • Certification in a design or multimedia-related discipline
  • Advanced understanding of UX/UCD/Usability principles, academic or on-the-job experience
  • An online portfolio that demonstrates your ability to articulate your process or ideas
  • Significant experience as a principal designer, or as a leader of a design team
  • Experience with UX research/analysis tools such as Loopback, Usability Hub, Typeform, etc.
  • Experience running workshops with project stakeholders to identify user needs and insights
  • Experience using UX/UI design software such as Sketch, Axure, Invision, etc.
  • Familiar with design systems, styleguides, grid-based design, and development handover
  • Proven history of providing design strategy and direction, must be comfortable giving and receiving constructive design feedback and be able to approach clients with a positive attitude when providing solutions and suggestions
  • Good command of Mandarin (written and spoken) is beneficial as some of our clients are based in China

This position is based in Singapore. Only candidates with unrestricted work rights for Singapore will be considered.

Motion Graphic Designer

south

Motion Graphic Designers design and create animated video productions for our clients.

As a Motion Graphic Designer, you will work with our creative and content teams to translate client messaging from script to storyboard. You will be expected to create and combine animation, audio, voice-over and effects to produce engaging video productions and campaigns. This role involves working as part of the studio design team based in our Singapore office.

Responsibilities

  • Collaborate with clients and internal/external teams to produce beautiful animated videos
  • Work with creative direction and copywriters to create eye-catching storyboards
  • Manipulate raw footage, illustrations and typography with flare and style
  • Work with the Creative Team to create enticing motion design that enhances campaigns
  • Come up with ideas and initiatives independently
  • Manage time effectively, multi-tasking to meet deadlines whilst maintaining high quality output
  • Communicate ideas, giving and receiving constructive feedback and be able to approach clients with a positive attitude when providing solutions and suggestions
  • Stay up-to-date on the latest graphic trends and help to establish and promote best practices within our organization

Requirements

  • Certification in a design or multimedia-related discipline
  • An online portfolio that demonstrates effective use of animation, visual effects, editing and video production
  • Expert knowledge of After Effects, Premier Pro, Audition and Video Compression Software is essential
  • Must be a team player and be able to work independently with minimal direction or supervision
  • Must be comfortable managing multiple projects simultaneously in a fast-paced environment
  • Must have experience delivering animated banners/graphics in a range of formats (e.g. gif)
  • Experience using 3D motion design or Game Design (e.g. Cinema 4D, Maya, Unity) is beneficial
  • Experience in HTML5 animation is beneficial
  • Good command of Mandarin (written and spoken) is beneficial

This position is based in Singapore. Only candidates with unrestricted work rights for Singapore will be considered.
